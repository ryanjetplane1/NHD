How much does a nuke cost?

According to the infographic the cost of a nuclear weapon can vary depending on various factors such as the country producing it, the size of the weapon, and the associated research and development costs. It is difficult to provide an exact figure, but estimates suggest that the cost of a single nuclear warhead can range from several million dollars to potentially hundreds of millions of dollars. Additionally, the ongoing maintenance and operational costs of nuclear arsenals add to the overall expenses.
